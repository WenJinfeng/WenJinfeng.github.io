I am now a PhD student at the Software Engineering Institute, Department of Computer Science and Technology, School of Electronics Engineering and Computer Science (EECS), Peking University. I major in Computer Software and Theory. I am supervised by Prof. Xuanzhe Liu. I received my BS and MS degrees from the Department of Computer Science and Technology, China University of Geosciences Beijing in 2017 and 2020, respectively.
