Jinfeng Wen(温 金凤)

I am now a PhD student at the Software Engineering Institute, Department of Computer Science and Technology, School of Electronics Engineering and Computer Science (EECS), Peking University. I am supervised by Prof. Xuanzhe Liu. I received my BS and MS degrees from the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Chine University of Geosciences Beijing in 2017 and 2020, respectively. My research interests mainly focus on serverless computing, program analysis, and service computing.
